Recipe For Preparing Sandwich

  1. Take two slices of brown bread

  2. With a Butter knife apply butter on it as required

  3. Put the stuffing of vegetables as per your likes (ex. Tomatoes, Cucumbers, Beetroot, etc.)

  4. Place it in a toaster and let it get toasted for 5-8 minutes

  5. Once crisp, remove from toaster and place it in a serving plate

  6. Garnish with chees and Tomato Ketchup

  7. ENJOY YOUR SANDWICH
